U.S.-IRAN PEACE DEAL & MIDDLE EAST TENSIONS
=========================================
* Strait of Hormuz: International Energy Agency (IEA) chief Fatih Birol emphasized that the unconditional reopening of the Strait of Hormuz to tanker traffic is vital to ending the global energy price shock. U.S. President Donald Trump has stated that the strait is partially open and is expected to be fully operational by Friday.
* Peace Agreement: Following the U.S.-Iran agreement to end the war, Iranian officials have indicated that the deal includes the withdrawal of Israeli forces from Lebanon. While the peace deal has been touted by President Trump at the G7 summit in France, it has faced criticism from Israeli Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu, who reaffirmed that Israel would prioritize its own security interests, specifically regarding Iran’s nuclear capabilities.
* Regional Cooperation: Iran announced plans to connect its electricity grid with Qatar’s, with further studies underway to link its power grid with other Gulf nations.
